Awakening of the Crimson Eye

A silent prowess. A gift of pain. A vision beyond vision.

The Sharingan

He hides his face, but not his power.
One eye shines in the darkest hour.
It’s called the Sharingan, glowing red,
A gift from a friend who once had bled.

It sees your moves before they show,
Reads your plans, your steps, and even your flow.
It copies thousand of techniques with a glance,
And gives ninja one more chance.

It can cast genjutsu to trick the mind,
Making you see things left behind.
It helps him move with speed and skill,
To win a fight with silent will.

He didn’t get it from his clan,
But still uses all he can.
A Friend's last gift from days of pain,
but still in his heart, and still remains.

Through pain and loss, he does not cry,
He watches with his crimson eye.
The Copy Ninja, calm and strong,
Protects the world where he belongs.
